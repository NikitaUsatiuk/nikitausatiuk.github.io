INDEPENDENT RESEARCH

IN SOCIAL SCIENCE

ESTABLISHMENT OF THE AUTOCEPHALOUS ORTHODOX CHURCH OF UKRAINE THROUGH THE PRISM OF PUBLIC OPINION: RELIGIOUS-SOCIOLOGICAL ANALYSIS

2021

In this study, I examined the influence of the foundation of the independent Orthodox Church on the political and social processes in Ukraine through the prism of the Presidental elections in 2019 in Ukraine.

It considers the issue of religiosity of Ukrainian society from two leading positions: the current religious self-identification and the implementation of obligatory religious practices by believers. Thus, the scientific optics' main focus was that in Ukrainian religious studies is called "ritual-domestic" or "every day" religious consciousness. Summing up, I was able to see that most Ukrainians declare their "cultural Orthodoxy." They refer to Orthodoxy as a sociocultural phenomenon but are not religious or ecclesiastical believers in the classical sense of the word.

IMPLEMENTATION OF INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGIES TO THE GOVERNMENTAL PROCESSES: SOCIAL ASPECT

In this research, I examined the attitude of Ukrainian society toward the digitization of governmental processes and the evolution of the concept of e-democracy in Ukraine.

After the triumph of Volodymyr Zelenskyy in the presidential race in 2019, he initiated global reforms in public administration through the widespread implementation of information and communication technologies to governmental processes. 

To ascertain more about the views of modern Ukrainians, I conducted my social survey, which demonstrated a clear correlation between the views of the respondents, political position, and their age group. Thus, young people who voted for Volodymyr Zelenskyy and who have progressive, liberal views supported such reforms, whereas representatives of the older generation, who have more conservative views and support the opponent of Zelenskyy, are radically against.

FORMATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF MONETARY SYSTEM IN SOVIET AND POST-SOVIET UKRAINE

2019

In this study, I examined the history of the creation and development of monetary relations in Ukraine in 1922-2019, based on my collection of banknotes and coins.

Money is not just an economic resource but a system of values and symbols that form and change along with society. Therefore, the monetary system is the best cut of the era, allowing you to look at it from a different angle. In this scientific study, I analyzed the basics of banknote symbolism during Soviet Ukraine, Ukraine during the Nazi occupation, and modern Ukraine.
